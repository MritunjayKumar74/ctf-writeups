# Sanity Check — GPNCTF

**Category:** Miscellaneous  
**Flag:** `GPNCTF{Yes Chef! I am ready for a fair competition}`

---

## Challenge Description

> Are all plates clean? The vegetables chopped? Is your work surface clean? The oven preheated and the fridge cold? Your head chef might ask these questions while we just ask if you've reviewed our rules.

---

## Solution

### Steps

1. Navigate to the **Rules** page of the GPNCTF competition.
2. Read the flag format section, which states:

   > *The flag format is `GPNCTF{.*}` (only printable ASCII characters). One example for a valid flag would be `GPNCTF{Yes Chef! I am ready for a fair competition}`*

3. The example flag provided **is** the actual flag. Copy and submit it.
